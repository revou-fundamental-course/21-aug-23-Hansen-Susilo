About Us

Based on our concern and passion for education, and our hope of Indonesia's bright future, Susilo's Superb School is committed to building a better future, both nationally and globally, through the cultivation of education and technology, whilst producing human recources with good quality, such as integrity, responsibility, social awareness, creativity, and leadership.

Vision & Mission
